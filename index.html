Questions that may be on your mind

Finding the right therapist is about connection and comfort. I offer a brief introductory call to see if we're a good fit. It's important you feel safe and understood. Trust your intuition during this initial conversation.

You can schedule an appointment by clicking the "Book a Session" button, which will allow you to email me directly. We can then coordinate a time that works for both of us.

The first session is primarily for us to get to know each other. We'll discuss what brought you to therapy, your history, and what you hope to achieve. It's a collaborative process where we set the foundation for our work together. You can share as much or as little as you feel comfortable with.

Most clients start with weekly sessions to build momentum. However, the frequency is flexible and can be adjusted to bi-weekly or monthly based on your needs, progress, and financial situation. We will decide this together.

Payment details will be discussed during our initial contact. I accept various forms of payment, including online bank transfers and UPI, for your convenience.

I require at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a fee. This policy allows me to offer the time slot to another client in need.
